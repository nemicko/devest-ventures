Our Approach

Utility, Not Speculation

Blockchain requires tokens to function—the question is what you do with them. At DeVest, we invest in platforms using tokens for real business utility: payments, transfers, access control, and programmable contracts. Not for speculation, hype, or digital collectibles.

We partner with founders building sustainable business models where blockchain solves actual problems more efficiently than traditional systems. Our focus is measurable value creation, not market sentiment.

Portfolio Companies

orbit.channel
Media & Content

Orbit Channel

A decentralized media distribution platform that enables creators, brands, and organizations to publish and monetize digital content directly, without intermediaries taking large commissions or controlling distribution rights.

Problem

Traditional content platforms extract high fees, control distribution, and limit direct ownership between creators and their audiences.

Solution

Smart contract-based distribution allowing creators to retain ownership while enabling programmable monetization through direct sales and tokenized access.

Business Model

Platform transaction fees on content sales, NFT releases, and tokenized memberships, plus premium analytics and distribution tools.

Content Platform Creator Economy Smart Contracts
travel-token.net
Travel & Hospitality

Travel Token

A blockchain-based booking and payment platform for travel services that uses smart contracts to facilitate reservations and payments for accommodation, experiences, and tourism products.

Problem

Traditional travel platforms charge excessive commission fees (15-25%), delay payments to service providers, and lack transparency in booking agreements.

Solution

Direct bookings with automated smart-contract payments that reduce transaction costs and provide immediate settlement to service providers.

Business Model

Low booking fees, token transaction fees, and premium services for travel providers including listing promotion, analytics, and integrated payment infrastructure.

Travel Tech Payments B2B Platform
